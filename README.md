## Project Description

This application allows you to effortlessly watch your favorite videos on YouTube. Imagine wanting to watch the music videos of some tracks from your Spotify playlist. Instead of remembering and searching for each song, you can simply copy the link to your favorite or specialized playlists, paste it into this website, and voilÃ ! You'll be redirected to YouTube with all your songs queued up and waiting for you!

## How to Use

1. Copy the link to your favorite or specialized Spotify playlist.
2. Paste the link into the provided field on our website.
3. Hit 'Submit', and get redirected to YouTube with your playlist queued up.
